## 🎮 How to Use the Main Options

### Aimbot

Use aimbot settings to change how the tool helps with aim.

Common options include:

- Aim key
- FOV
- Smoothness
- Target selection
- Hitbox choice

A lower FOV keeps the aim help tighter. A higher smoothness makes movement less sharp.

### ESP

ESP shows useful info about other players and map objects.

You may see:

- Player boxes
- Health bars
- Names
- Distance
- Weapons
- Bomb info

Turn on only the parts you want so the screen stays easy to read.

### Recoil Control

Recoil control helps keep your shots steady during spray.

Common controls:

- Strength
- Horizontal control
- Vertical control
- Weapon presets

Start with low settings and adjust until the feel matches your play style.

### Triggerbot

Triggerbot fires when your crosshair lands on a target.

Useful settings:

- Activation key
- Delay
- Hitbox filter
- Team check

A small delay can make the action feel more natural.

### Skin Changer

The skin changer updates how your weapons look in game.

Typical options include:

- Weapon skin selection
- Knife model
- Glove style
- Wear level
- StatTrak toggle
- Seed and pattern settings

Apply the change, then reload your loadout or rejoin the match if needed.

### Bomb Timer

The bomb timer shows how much time remains on the planted bomb.

This helps you plan retakes and exits with less guesswork.

### Gun Changer

Gun changer options let you swap the look or model of weapons.

Use this section to set a preferred style for each weapon type.
